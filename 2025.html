The 8th Advanced School on Cryptology and Information Security in Latin America (ASCrypto) will be co-located with LatinCrypt2025 on the 29th and 30rd of September, 2025, in Medellín, Colombia.


Chairs:

  • Arantxa Zapico, Cryptography Researcher, Ethereum Foundation
  • Javier Verbel Cryptography Researcher, Technology Innovation Institute

Topic:

Modern Proof Systems: This year, the school will focus on cutting-edge topics in modern proving systems, including recursion, STARKs, and MPC-in-the-head—areas that are gaining increasing attention within the global research community due to their real-world and theoretical applications.

Confirmed Speakers:

1. Arantxa Zapico Ethereum Foundation.
2. Benedikt Bünz New York University.
3. Sophia Yakoubov Aarhus University.
