The 8th Advanced School on Cryptology and Information Security in Latin America (ASCrypto) will be co-located with LatinCrypt 2025, taking place on September 29–30, 2025 in Medellín, Colombia.



Topic: Modern Proof Systems

This year’s edition of ASCrypto will focus on advanced topics in modern proving systems, including recursion, STARKs, and MPC-in-the-head. These areas are gaining significant traction in both theoretical research and practical applications.

Confirmed Speakers

  • Arantxa Zapico, Ethereum Foundation
  • Benedikt Bünz, New York University
  • Sophia Yakoubov, Aarhus University

Chairs

  • Arantxa Zapico, Cryptography Researcher, Ethereum Foundation
  • Javier Verbel, Cryptography Researcher, Technology Innovation Institute
